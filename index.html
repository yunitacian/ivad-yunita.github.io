RESUME

Hi, my name is GuYinuo.
I'm a Visual Communication Designer
Interaction Designer
Media Scholar and more.


Being trained as a visual communication designer at Zhejiang University, I am currently a junior student, daily learning includes courses related to posters, packaging, branding, and interaction. During the learning process, I gradually developed an interest in interaction design, and hoped to convey information to the audience through media that are not limited to screens and paper.

New means of media making are being invented and reinvented as we speak, and new interactions made possible by these mediums deeply fascinate me. I believe in the power of media and interaction in channeling meaningful connection, and aspire to create designs that reveal the unseen in our “everyday” for introspection and growth.
